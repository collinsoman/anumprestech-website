Welcome to Anum Prestech

Empowering students through quality education in a disciplined environment

Boarding Facilities

Our boarding program provides a safe and structured environment for students to grow academically, socially, and spiritually. Supervised study sessions, nutritious meals, and dedicated house staff ensure the wellbeing and success of every boarder.

Day Student Life

Day students enjoy full access to academic and extracurricular activities while returning home each evening. With flexible transport arrangements and close ties with parents, our day system fosters strong community engagement.

Speech about Anum Prestech Boarding House

Welcome To Anum Presteh everyone,

It is an absolute joy to speak about a place that many of us proudly call our second home - Anum Prestech Boarding House.

Here, we don't just live; we grow. We learn discipline, unity, and responsibility. From the morning wake-up bell to lights out, every moment shapes us into better students and stronger individuals.

Our boarding house is not just about rules and routines - it's about friendship, laughter, support, and shared dreams. Whether it's evening prep, house meetings, or weekend cleanups, we do it together - as a family.

At Anum Prestech Boarding House, we build character. We build confidence. And most importantly, we build a community where everyone belongs.
